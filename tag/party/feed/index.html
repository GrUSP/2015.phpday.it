On Friday 15th evening as an attendee you’re welcome to the phpDay social night sponsored by Trivago at “Pizzeria San Marco” where you’ll find pizza, pasta, and drinks. Free beer, you know?

All attendees will find four tickets in their goodie bags: one for the entry, and three for drinks.

Reaching the social night venue is extremely simple as you can walk there from Hotel San Marco in 7 minutes.

There will be food available for meat lovers and vegetarians (lacto-ovo-vegetarians) alike; if you want vegan food, just ask to the waiters and the restaurant will cook vegan food for you.

See you there!
